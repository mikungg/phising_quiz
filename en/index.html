Welcome to the Nabu Phishing Quiz

Phishing is an online fraud technique that aims to steal personal data or login credentials by impersonating a trusted party. Beware of suspicious links or attachments, check the sender's address, and don't provide sensitive information without verifying the source's authenticity.
